Determining Clusters of Celebrities using Twitter data

Can we determine what profession a given celebrity occupies, solely based on tweets mentioning that celebrity? A partner and I collected weeks of Twitter data, created a unigram frequency vector for each celebrity, and clustered using the k-means algorithm. We performed this on 100 celebrities in four different professions: Acting, Sports, Music, and Politics, with fairly promising results.
